Why I Like Dogs

Dogs

I like dogs because they are friendly, loyal, and full of energy. Dogs can make people feel happy just by wagging their tail or running around with excitement. They enjoy playing outside, going for walks, and spending time with their owners. Every dog has a different personality, which makes them fun to learn about. Some dogs are calm and gentle, while others are playful and silly. Dogs can also be very smart and learn tricks or commands. Being around dogs makes me feel relaxed and cheerful because they always seem happy to see you.

  • They are loyal
